# CONTROLLED BETA DEMO ONE-PAGER

## 1-Line Positioning
ParselRadar is a controlled-beta intelligence workflow that structures parcel evidence and risk signals into a disciplined, repeatable review process.

## Who It Is For
- Early controlled-beta users who need structured parcel review workflows.
- Property professionals and disciplined buyers/investors who value evidence tracking.

## Current Value
- Turns scattered parcel checks into a clear intake -> evidence -> report workflow.
- Makes missing evidence and next actions explicit.
- Improves consistency of review conversations across stakeholders.

## What Works Now
- End-to-end guided flow is live and demoable.
- Documents/evidence guidance is explicit and trust-safe.
- Result/report matrix provides practical next actions.
- Connector center governance boundaries are visible and understandable.

- Official source checks remain manual unless permissioned integrations are approved.
