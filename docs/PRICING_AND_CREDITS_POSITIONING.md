# PRICING AND CREDITS POSITIONING

## Model Summary
ParselRadar uses a credit-based access model for informational pre-check workflows.

Credits are used to support:
- property/listing pre-check flow
- evidence organization
- missing-evidence/readiness review
- report preparation context before professional verification

Final public pricing can be configured before public launch.

## Buyer Use Cases
Credits help buyers and investors:
- screen a land/property opportunity earlier
- upload listing, parcel, and supporting evidence
- identify missing documents
- prepare better questions for municipality, TKGM, and professionals

## Professional Use Cases
Credits support professional-oriented preparation by enabling:
- cleaner intake packages
- evidence completeness checks
- review-ready case preparation
- future pre-qualified lead workflows only with user consent

## What Credits Do Not Guarantee
Credits do not provide or guarantee:
- official legal proof
- official tapu/cadastral/zoning confirmation
- official valuation proof
- replacement for licensed legal/professional due diligence

## Public Claim Boundaries
ParselRadar is informational and pre-check focused.

ParselRadar does not claim:
- fake official-proof status
- live official TKGM/TUCBS/CSB automation where unavailable
- public-launch readiness while launch gates are incomplete

## Launch and Payment Status Boundaries
Public launch remains NOT_READY.
SMTP/DNS readiness remains CONFIG_REQUIRED.

Payment/pricing configuration may be refined before public launch, but boundaries and disclaimer language must remain explicit.
