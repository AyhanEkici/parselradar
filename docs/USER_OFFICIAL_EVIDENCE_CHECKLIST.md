# User Official Evidence Checklist

## Before Upload
- Is this document about the correct parcel?
- Does it show il, ilce, mahalle, ada, and parsel?
- Is the source visible?
- Is the date visible?
- Are sensitive identity details redacted?
- Is the document readable?
- Is the document complete enough for preliminary analysis?

## Accepted Evidence Categories
- parcel screenshots
- zoning or imar plan screenshots
- municipality documents
- listing screenshots
- title or tapu-related documents
- location or map screenshots
- infrastructure evidence
- photos
- seller or agent documents

## Not Accepted as Official Proof
- WhatsApp-only claims
- verbal seller statements
- edited images without source
- screenshots without parcel reference
- incomplete cropped documents

## Labeling Reminder
Uploaded materials are treated as USER_PROVIDED_OFFICIAL_EVIDENCE unless validated by an approved connector.
