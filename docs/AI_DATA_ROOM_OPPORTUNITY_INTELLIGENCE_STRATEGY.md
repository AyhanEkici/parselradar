# AI Data Room Opportunity Intelligence Strategy

## Scope
This strategy defines how future ParselRadar AI chat can search and explain opportunities while respecting legal source boundaries and user isolation.

## AI May Search
- ParselRadar database
- uploaded documents
- extracted text
- public/open data
- user-provided evidence
- admin-reviewed evidence
- approved connector data later

## AI Must Not
- log in to e-Devlet
- use personal credentials
- scrape authenticated pages
- invent missing parcel facts
- present UCBP/TUCBS evidence as official legal proof
- leak cross-user data
- ignore source labels

## AI Chat Use Cases
- find best opportunities under budget
- rank lowest TL/m2 parcels
- identify missing evidence
- compare districts/provinces
- flag high-risk listings
- cluster opportunities by region
- produce admin shortlist
- explain why each opportunity is strong or weak

## Required AI Answer Rules
- cite source record/document
- show confidence score
- show missing evidence
- show risk flags
- separate fact vs inference
- never claim official legal/tapu/imar certainty

## Data Isolation and Governance
- AI responses must remain tenant/user scoped.
- Cross-user data exposure is forbidden.
- Source labels must be preserved in retrieval and response rendering.
- AI ranking output is decision support, not legal certification or investment advice.
