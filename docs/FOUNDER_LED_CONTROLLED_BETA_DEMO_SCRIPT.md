# FOUNDER-LED CONTROLLED BETA DEMO SCRIPT

## 1) Demo Objective
- Show ParselRadar as a controlled-beta intelligence workflow for disciplined parcel review.
- Demonstrate what works now end-to-end: intake -> evidence -> report.
- Set correct trust boundaries: guidance-first, no official verification claims.
- Leave the viewer with confidence in practical beta value without overclaiming.

## 2) Audience
- Early controlled-beta viewers: property professionals, disciplined individual buyers/investors, and pilot partners.
- Stakeholders who need structured review workflows, not automated government decisions.
- Decision-makers evaluating process quality, repeatability, and trust posture.

## 3) What To Say Before Starting
- This is a controlled-beta intelligence workflow.
- The report is guidance, not official verification.
- Official source checks remain manual unless permissioned integrations are approved.
- The system helps structure evidence and risk signals.
- This is designed to make parcel review more disciplined and repeatable.
- Some metadata completeness is still being hardened before public launch.
- Public launch status is NOT_READY; controlled beta status is READY_FOR_CONTROLLED_BETA_CONTINUATION.

## 4) What Not To Claim
- Do not claim official verification.
- Do not claim official valuation.
- Do not claim automated TKGM verification.
- Do not claim automated e-Imar verification.
- Do not claim buy/sell recommendations.
- Do not claim public-launch readiness.
- Do not claim fully automated government data coverage.

## 5) 10-Minute Demo Flow
1. Expectations (1 min): state controlled-beta boundaries and guidance-only posture.
2. Dashboard (1 min): show entry points and workflow map.
3. New property intake (2 min): show structured input and draft logic.
4. Property detail (1 min): show current state, trust disclaimers, and analysis context.
5. Documents/evidence (2 min): show manual-source guidance and upload expectations.
6. Result/report (2 min): show readiness summary, evidence matrix, missing evidence actions.
7. Connector center close (1 min): show governance boundaries and metadata-only sync posture.

## 7) Route-by-Route Talking Points
### Homepage
- Positioning is evidence-first and trust-constrained.
- Emphasize this is not a public-launch claim surface.

### Login
- Entry gate to controlled beta workspace.
- Mention role-based internal/pilot usage context.

- Single place to start intake, review reports, and navigate evidence-driven flow.
- Highlight operational clarity instead of hype.

### New Property Intake
- Structured capture reduces ad-hoc evaluation mistakes.
- Explain that this improves consistency, not official decision automation.

### Report/Result

- Missing evidence prompts are workflow controls, not legal determinations.

### Report Alias

### Connector Center

- Manual/blocked sources are intentionally skipped for legal/trust reasons.

### Credits
- Credits support workflow usage scope; not tied to official proof outcomes.

### Map
- Preview context with transparent provider constraints.
- Diagnostic visibility is part of trust posture.
