# PRODUCT STRATEGY AND MARKET ARCHITECTURE

## 1. Executive Positioning
ParselRadar is a Turkish real-estate evidence and deal-intelligence workspace focused on pre-due-diligence support.

Core line:

> Check Turkish land, parcels and property opportunities before you buy.

Positioning definition:
- Turkish real-estate evidence and deal-intelligence workspace
- Pre-due-diligence support platform
- Not an official tapu/imar/legal verification authority

What ParselRadar does:
- Helps users collect and organize evidence before a purchase or advisory decision
- Shows missing checks and next evidence actions
- Improves readiness for safer professional and official follow-up

What ParselRadar does not do:
- Does not issue official legal, tapu, cadastral, zoning, or valuation proof
- Does not replace licensed experts or government institutions

## 2. Target User Groups

### Individual Buyers/Investors
Pain point:
- Incomplete listing data and fragmented evidence before purchase decisions

What they upload:
- Listing screenshots, ada/parsel context, TKGM visuals, municipality evidence, PDFs/photos

What ParselRadar gives:
- Evidence organization, missing-evidence guidance, readiness visibility, pre-check structure

What still needs official/professional verification:
- Tapu records, municipality zoning legality, encumbrances, legal due diligence

Monetization potential:
- Credits, paid report/PDF, consumer plan upgrades

### Diaspora Buyers
Pain point:
- Remote buying risk, local verification uncertainty, language/process gaps

What they upload:
- Agent-shared listings, location details, screenshots, exported documents

What ParselRadar gives:
- Structured evidence workspace and guided next-step checklist across distance

What still needs official/professional verification:
- Local legal checks, notarized/official workflows, municipality/tapu confirmations

Monetization potential:
- Premium remote-buyer package, multilingual support add-ons, paid report bundles

### Real Estate Agents
Pain point:
- Pre-qualification is manual and inconsistent across listings/clients

What they upload:
- Listing sets, property evidence packs, client-supplied documents

What ParselRadar gives:
- Faster pre-check packaging, evidence completeness signals, cleaner client prep

What still needs official/professional verification:
- Final compliance/legal claims and official institution validation

Monetization potential:
- Agency subscriptions, team seats, listing pipeline tools

### Developers / Muteahhit
Pain point:
- Early opportunity filtering is noisy and evidence collection is slow

What they upload:
- Parcel context, zoning-related screenshots/documents, market-signal evidence

What ParselRadar gives:
- Opportunity pre-screening, evidence readiness queue, deal triage support

What still needs official/professional verification:
- Development rights, zoning legitimacy, engineering/legal feasibility

Monetization potential:
- Developer dashboard plans, portfolio monitoring, pro workflows

### Architects / Imar Consultants
Pain point:
- Intake quality varies; project kickoff often starts with missing critical documents

What they upload:
- Planning/e-plan evidence, site context files, municipality-related documents

What ParselRadar gives:
- Intake standardization, evidence gap visibility, reusable pre-check templates

What still needs official/professional verification:
- Final design approvals, official permits, municipality-authorized outputs

Monetization potential:
- Professional workspace subscriptions, client intake pipelines

### Legal / Due-Diligence Professionals
Pain point:
- Clients arrive with scattered or inconsistent evidence

What they upload:
- Client-provided legal support documents and property evidence bundles

What ParselRadar gives:
- Better case preparation structure and evidence completeness baseline

What still needs official/professional verification:
- Legal opinions, formal risk clearance, institution-backed confirmations

Monetization potential:
- Professional review upsell, firm-level plans, referral and lead model (with consent)

### Platform Operator/Admin
Pain point:
- Need controlled quality, policy compliance, and safe scaling from beta

What they upload/manage:
- Governance templates, review metadata, operational runbooks

What ParselRadar gives:
- Admin observability, controlled moderation, evidence governance surface

What still needs official/professional verification:
- Any externally binding official decision or legal certification

Monetization potential:
- SaaS revenue optimization, marketplace governance fees (future, consent-based)

## 3. Jobs-To-Be-Done

Before buying:
- Collect and structure evidence for a property
- Detect missing checks before commitment
- Prepare a safer next-step package for official/professional verification

Before listing:
- Assemble defensible evidence set for buyer trust
- Identify and close obvious information gaps

Before developing:
- Filter opportunities by evidence readiness and planning context
- Prioritize parcels requiring deeper professional review

Before advising a client:
- Build standardized intake package quickly
- Reduce time spent requesting missing documents repeatedly

Before sharing a deal:
- Share only consented, relevant evidence
- Present a clear pre-check status without overclaiming

## 4. Evidence Hub Model
Supported evidence inputs:
- Listing/ad data
- Ada/parsel identity
- TKGM screenshots/exports
- TKGM price-history/analysis screenshots as market signal
- Municipality/e-Imar/e-Plan/imar durum evidence
- Photos, PDFs, KML, GeoJSON, CSV
- Market comparable signals

Evidence meaning:
- Supporting informational evidence only
- Designed for pre-check, structuring, and readiness improvement
- Not an automatic or official proof mechanism

## 5. Missing Evidence Guidance Model
Verified loop:

missing evidence -> where to get it -> what to capture/download -> upload with intent -> return to result -> readiness improves

Operational value:
- Reduces ambiguity around next action
- Preserves intent-aware upload context
- Increases consistency of evidence collection for user and admin workflows

## 6. Professional Value Model
Professional use cases:
- Intake dashboard for incoming client/property cases
- Evidence completeness tracking
- Client report preparation baseline
- Pre-qualified lead identification
- Region/deal monitoring
- Developer opportunity queue
- Market signal aggregation

Professional outcome:
- More structured intake
- Faster early-stage filtering
- Better client communication before formal verification stages

## 7. Consent-Based Deal-Flow Model
Principles:
- User opt-in required for any deal-flow or lead sharing
- Anonymized or permissioned sharing only
- No hidden backdoor use of user data
- Admin/operator may match users/properties with professionals only under consent
- Positioning: lead/deal intelligence support, not forced brokerage routing

## 8. Revenue Model
Revenue building blocks:
- Credits per analysis
- Paid PDF/report
- Pro subscription

- Lead/referral marketplace only with consent
- Professional review upsell

Commercial logic:
- Consumer flow monetized by usage and reports
- Professional flow monetized by subscriptions and workflow productivity
- Marketplace flow gated by consent and policy controls
