# HOMEPAGE REDESIGN BRIEF

## Homepage Goal
Create a clear, high-trust public entry page that positions ParselRadar as an evidence-first pre-due-diligence workspace for Turkish real-estate decisions.

## Primary CTA
- Start your property pre-check

## Secondary CTA
- See sample report and evidence workflow

## Target Audiences
- Individual buyers/investors
- Diaspora buyers
- Real-estate agents
- Developers and consultants
- Legal/due-diligence professionals

## Page Sections
1. Hero
2. Problem framing
3. How it works
4. What you can check
5. Evidence needed
6. For buyers
7. For professionals
8. Report preview
9. Trust and disclaimer
10. Pricing and credits
11. Final CTA

## Trust/Disclaimer Copy
Required trust message:
- ParselRadar provides informational pre-check guidance and evidence organization.
- ParselRadar is not an official legal/tapu/imar verification authority.
- Final official verification must be completed with authorized institutions and professionals.

## Conversion Flow
1. Visitor lands on homepage
2. Understands product scope and boundaries
3. Starts pre-check flow via CTA
4. Uploads listing/parcel/evidence
5. Receives missing-evidence guidance and readiness signal
6. Proceeds to paid analysis/report options

## What Not To Claim
- Do not claim official legal proof
- Do not claim official tapu or zoning confirmation
- Do not claim live automated TKGM/TUCBS/CSB integration if unavailable
- Do not claim official valuation certification
- Do not imply public-launch readiness while launch gates remain incomplete

## Design Tone
- Professional
- Evidence-first
- Turkish real-estate intelligence oriented
- Not government-looking
- Not overpromising

## Suggested Hero Copy
Headline:

> Check Turkish land, parcels and property opportunities before you buy.

Subline:

> Upload a listing, parcel details or evidence. ParselRadar helps you organize source documents, identify missing checks and prepare a safer next step.
