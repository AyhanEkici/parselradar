# UCBP Manual Evidence Capture Standard

## Purpose
Standardize how admin captures manual UCBP/e-Devlet evidence safely for city/province opportunity research.

## Capture Standard
- capture only required planning/opportunity context
- keep province/district/mahalle/location references
- include which layers were checked
- include date/time and observation summary
- redact identity and sensitive session information

## Mandatory Redaction
- do not include T.C. Kimlik values
- do not include e-Devlet username/password
- do not include SMS/MFA codes
- do not include session tokens/cookies
- do not include full personal contact details unless legally required and minimized

## Allowed Evidence Labels
- ADMIN_MANUAL_OBSERVATION
- ACCESS_CONTROLLED_INFORMATIONAL_SOURCE
- PUBLIC_SOURCE
- USER_PROVIDED_OFFICIAL_EVIDENCE
- UNVERIFIED
- MISSING
- NEEDS_OFFICIAL_VERIFICATION

## Prohibited Claims
- no official legal/tapu/imar guarantee
- no statement that manual portal view equals approved connector proof
- no statement that opportunity is legally verified without independent approved source

## Governance Boundary
Manual evidence improves opportunity intelligence but does not replace approved connector validation or legal due diligence.
